About Us

This is a Music Website made from HTML, CSS and Javascript. It consists of 10 random songs in a scrollable list and the ability to play/pause them using Javascript. It also has the ability to play the previous/next track and the ability to seek the timeframe of songs with the help of a bar.

You can use the spacebar to pause/play the song, right arrow key to move forward, left arrow to move backwards, < to play the previous song and > to play the next one.

The website is responsive.
